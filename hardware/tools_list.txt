Hardware Components List
- ESP32 DevKit v1 (main controller)
- Arduino Nano (sensor node)
- MPU6050 (6-axis IMU)
- SHTC3 (temperature & humidity sensor)
- TOF range sensor (distance measurement)
- RGB USB camera (fire detection)
- Thermal USB camera (heat confirmation)
- Pan servo motor (continuous rotation)
- Tilt servo motor (positional)
- ESC x2 (weapon/suppressor control)
- Joystick (manual pan/tilt input)
- Push button (arm/disarm control)
- Relay modules x3
- Voltage regulators
- MOSFETs / logic level shifters
